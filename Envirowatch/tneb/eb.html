EV CORPORATE PROFILE

  • On 1st July 1957, Tamil Nadu Electricity Board and has remained the energy provider and distributor all these years. After 53 years of journey the Tamil Nadu Electricity Board a holding company with the name "TNEB Ltd" and two subsidiary companies namely "Tamil Nadu Transmission Corporation Ltd.,"(TANTRANSCO) and "Tamil Nadu Generation and Distribution Corporation Ltd.," (TANGEDCO) as per the mandatory requirements of the Electricity Act 2003.
